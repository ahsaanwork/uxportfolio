Nightingale Capital Temp

Nightingale Capital is a forward thinking real estate company that has been around for over 5 years. They partnered with us to create a modern, impactful visual identity and a wide reaching design system that stands out in a sea of sameness within an industry that doesn't traditionally prioritize design.

Overview

Learning from the past to build the future

The road to somewhere new isn’t always smooth sailing. Our journey toward building Corvel’s new visual identity had its fair share of bumps along the way. We overcame challenge after challenge in collaboration with a client who was resistant to conventional brand practices, successfully delivering a tailored solution that aligns with their unique preferences while maintaining functionality and brand coherence.

By utilizing their legacy brand elements in new ways and pairing them with modern design motifs, we crafted a confident yet personable visual language that sets them apart from their competitors and clearly communicates their commitment to providing outstanding services and superior technology.

Digital Assets

We began by developing a robust design system for their website, which was then utilized to enhance their case management platform and create various campaign landing pages.

Printed Matter

For print assets we developed an additional headline treatment to increase visual hierarchy, this provided more flexibility for various applications and headline lengths.
